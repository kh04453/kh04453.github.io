About Me

Carolanne Horne

I have been a stylist since 2015. I worked in Statesboro, GA for a year before moving to Jacksonville, FL to pursue new opportunities. I have a passion for coloring using balayage to create both modest and extreme enhancements. I enjoy taking classes to expand my abilities and keep up with the latest trends and techniques. I love connecting with my clients, both on a personal and professional level. I believe this is the key to quality service, customer satisfaction, and a world-class salon experience.
